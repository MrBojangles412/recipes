this is a recipe for poo pie.

ingredients:

1 premade pie crust - can be purchased at your local grocer
7 ears of corn
1 lb shelled peanuts

Instructions:

prepare the corn by either grilling or boiling.  Cut the kernels off the cooked ears of corn and mix them together with the peanuts.  Once the mixture is evenly distributed eat it all.  The next day collect the corn and peanut poop and place it in the pie crust.  Best if served hot but can be served chilled.  Eat a bottle of siracha for a spicy surprise...
